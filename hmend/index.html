Our Company

What we believe

Scenery upgrade offers professional exterior cleaning services since 2006 to your home or business to help keep it looking great.

Scenery upgrade prides itself on customer satisfaction.

Paying close attention to detail and providing cleaning services tailored to the specific needs of our clients. We believe in offering all of our customers professional and timely services. Our licensed and insured technician will make sure that only the finest and safest biodegradable chemicals are used on the client's surfaces. We offer bleach free products that are designed to clean houses and without killing plants. Environmental integrity is extremely important to us therefore great care and consideration goes into selecting products that are safe for our clients, landscape and their pets.
