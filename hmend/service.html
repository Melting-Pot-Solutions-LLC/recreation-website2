Gutter cleaning

You already know what it’s like to have gutters filled with pine straw, leaves and other debris. Every time it rains, your clogged gutters overflow, sending dirty, stagnant rainwater down onto your head and neck while you fumble for your house keys.

Clean gutters and downspouts move rainwater away from your roof and eaves, disposing of it neatly. When gutters work efficiently, they prevent water from backing up onto the roof, keeping it saturated and predisposed to rot. Clean gutters also keep rainwater from overflowing and cutting ruts in your landscaping.

Concrete/stone cleaning

If your property has historic, natural stone retaining walls, steps or rock trim, it needs special care to clean and bring out the stones’ subtle coloring. So what if your stonework isn’t historic yet? It could be someday, if you hire us to clean and preserve the integrity of your masonry.

Then there’s concrete. When moss or black algae build up on concrete patios and sidewalks, these surfaces become slippery and quite possibly hazardous to your health and wallet. But be wary of any old cleaner…

Concrete looks very durable. But improper cleaning materials or methods can create pockmarks and discoloration. Pressure washing can also degrade concrete by loosing joints and widening cracks.

We have the experience you need to safely make your concrete and stonework sparkle again.
